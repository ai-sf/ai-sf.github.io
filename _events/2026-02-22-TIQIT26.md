The Trento-Innsbruck Quantum Information Tour is an event organised by AISF Trento (Local Committee of the Italian Association of Physics Students AISF) and Spettro di Povo, in collaboration with AISF, IQOQI and the universities of Trento and Innsbruck: it will bring 40 enthusiastic students to visit some of the most advanced research facilities in Trento and Innsbruck and is conceived to showcase research mainly in the field of Quantum Information and Quantum Science.

### Who

40 Physics students members of AISF (Associazione Italiana Studenti di Fisica) or IAPS (International Association of Physics Students), among whom:

- 8 students from the Innsbruck University
- 8 students from the University of Trento
- 24 students from other universities

### Where

The first three days will be spent in Innsbruck at the IQOQI research center and at the Innsbruck University, the last three in Trento visiting the University of Trento and the FBK labs
